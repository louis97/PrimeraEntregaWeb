NewSpark

Fighting bias

NewSpark promotes interest in political news, which is affected by their bias. We believe that society can be positively impacted by encouraging people to get involved in a participatory democracy to inform themselves with awareness. Through our partiality thermometer we make people aware of the subjectivity contained in the texts before they read them; thus the bias is blurred, so that the power is not in the persuasion of the writer, but in the criterion of the reader.
